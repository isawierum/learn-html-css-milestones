Colors, ranked

  1. Purple

    • superior
    • beautiful
    • lavender tones are especially soothing

  2. Blue

    • every shade is pleasing in different ways
    • bright blue is the prettiest

  3. Red

    • the color of life
    • fills me with joy and excitement

  4. Yellow

    • at it's best, wonderful, joyful, golden sunshine which makes you feel warm inside
    • conversely, at it's worst, the color or mustard or urine

  5. Green

    • nothing especially positive or negative
    • it just sort of exists
    • still nice to look at

  6. Pink

    • acceptable and visually pleasing at times
    • can be harsh or obnoxious
    • sometimes fills me with rage because when I was young I was expected to like it because I was a girl

  7. Orange

    • the color of insanity
    • scourge of my existance
    • occasionally acceptable but often irritating
